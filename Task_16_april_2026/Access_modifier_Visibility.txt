Answer to Your Question:
Protected members are NOT accessible from different folders or 
different exports. Protected is specifically designed for inheritance 
hierarchies only. Even if you export/import a BaseClass from a different 
module or folder, trying to access its protected members will cause a
 TypeScript compilation error.
